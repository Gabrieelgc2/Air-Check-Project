AIR CHECK

The main goal of our project is to get credibility and learn more about Django. One of our website features is air quality monitoring, responsible for asking the user for a location to generate some informative data about the air and the amount of pollutants in their region. Our project was formed by these members: Gabriel Garcia, Marcello Fernandes, Vinicius Lima, Hugo Ponciano, Victor Monte, Larissa Lima, Lucas Ferreira, Lucas Bezerra and Thomas Barlavento.
